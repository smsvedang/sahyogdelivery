Refunds & Cancellations

Policy Overview

Last updated:

1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch confirmation. Once the order is marked as dispatched or out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be processed for cancelled orders, unavailable items, duplicate payments, or verified delivery/service issues.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may be denied for incorrect customer details, missed calls at delivery time, refusal to accept valid order, or products not eligible under applicable medicine regulations.

4. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are typically initiated within 5 to 10 working days and may vary based on bank/payment provider processing timelines.

5. Support for Claims

For cancellation/refund requests, contact Order Support with your tracking ID, order details, and reason for request.
