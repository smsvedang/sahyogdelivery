Ship & Delivery Policy

Sahyog Medical Delivery Platform

Last updated:

1. Service Coverage

We currently deliver within our active serviceable areas. Orders outside service range may be cancelled with notification to the customer.

2. Shipping and Delivery Charges

Order Type Delivery Charge Notes
Order value below Rs 500 Rs 30 Standard local delivery charge
Order value Rs 500 and above Free No delivery charge within service area
Urgent / express request Rs 60 Subject to delivery partner availability

Final charges, if any, are shown at order confirmation before dispatch.

3. Delivery Timeline

  • Standard delivery: Within 2 to 6 hours from order confirmation.
  • High-demand or peak slots: Up to 24 hours.
  • Out-of-stock/partially available items: Timeline shared by support after confirmation.

Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to traffic, weather, stock availability, and customer response time.

4. Failed Delivery Attempts

If the customer is unreachable, has an incorrect address, or does not respond at the time of delivery, re-delivery may be rescheduled and additional delivery charges can apply.

5. Customer Support

For shipping, delivery delay, or charge clarification, contact support with your Tracking ID and registered phone number.
